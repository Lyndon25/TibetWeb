Tour Overview

The ultimate Tibetan adventure. Journey from Lhasa to the foot of the world's highest peak. Stand at Everest Base Camp and witness the sunrise that has captivated explorers for generations.

Stand at Everest Base Camp (5,200m)
Sunrise over Everest from Rongbuk
Turquoise Yamdrok Lake
Tashilhunpo Monastery in Shigatse
Small group max 6 travelers

Daily Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Lhasa

Pickup and hotel check-in. Rest for acclimatization.

🍴 Dinner 🏨 Lhasa 4-star hotel
Day 2

Lhasa Sightseeing

Potala Palace and Jokhang Temple visit.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Lhasa 4-star hotel
Day 3

Lhasa to Shigatse

Drive via Yamdrok Lake, Karola Glacier, and Gyantse to Shigatse.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner 🏨 Shigatse 4-star hotel
Day 4

Shigatse to Everest

Drive to Everest Base Camp via Tingri. Overnight at Everest Town or Rongbuk area.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner 🏨 Guesthouse near EBC
Day 5

Everest Sunrise & Return

Early morning sunrise view of Everest. Drive back to Shigatse.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Shigatse 4-star hotel
Day 6

Shigatse to Lhasa

Visit Tashilhunpo Monastery, then drive back to Lhasa.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Lhasa 4-star hotel
Day 7

Namtso Lake Day Trip

Full day excursion to Namtso Lake, one of Tibet's three sacred lakes.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Lhasa 4-star hotel
Day 8

Lhasa Free Day

Free day to explore teahouses, shops, or revisit favorite spots. Optional cooking class.

🍴 Breakfast 🏨 Lhasa 4-star hotel
Day 9

Departure

Airport transfer. Tour concludes.

🍴 Breakfast 🏨 N/A
