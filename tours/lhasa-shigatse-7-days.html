Tour Overview

Journey from Lhasa to Shigatse through some of Tibet's most stunning landscapes. Marvel at turquoise Yamdrok Lake, ancient Gyantse, and the majestic Tashilhunpo Monastery.

Sacred Yamdrok Lake panorama

Scenic drive along Friendship Highway

Daily Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Lhasa

Airport pickup and hotel check-in. Rest for altitude acclimatization.

🍴 Dinner 🏨 Lhasa 4-star hotel
Day 2

Lhasa City Highlights

Potala Palace, Jokhang Temple, and Barkhor Street exploration.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Lhasa 4-star hotel
Day 3

Lhasa to Gyantse

Drive over Kamba La Pass for Yamdrok Lake view. Visit Karola Glacier and arrive in Gyantse.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner 🏨 Gyantse 3-star hotel
Day 4

Gyantse to Shigatse

Morning at Gyantse Kumbum and Pelkor Chode Monastery. Afternoon drive to Shigatse.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Shigatse 4-star hotel
Day 5

Shigatse Exploration

Visit Tashilhunpo Monastery, seat of the Panchen Lama. Explore local markets.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Shigatse 4-star hotel
Day 6

Return to Lhasa

Scenic drive back to Lhasa with stops for photos. Evening free in Lhasa.

🍴 Breakfast, Lunch 🏨 Lhasa 4-star hotel
Day 7

Departure

Airport or train station transfer. End of tour.
