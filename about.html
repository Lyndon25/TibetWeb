Our Story

TibetRide was born from a simple belief: Tibet should not be defined by rushed bus tours. We believe that a true Tibet journey needs local eyes, enough patience, and the flexibility that only a small group can offer.

Since 2016, we have led travelers from around the world into every corner of Tibet — from the prayer wheels of Lhasa's old town to the starry skies at Everest Base Camp, and the sacred kora around Mount Kailash. We are not a travel agency. We are Tibetans who love our home and want to show the real Tibet to the world.
