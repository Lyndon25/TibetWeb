果果塘茶园▲图源丨墨脱文旅

清晨的雅鲁藏布大峡谷还笼罩在云雾中， 墨脱县的高山茶园已是一片忙碌 ， 这片土地雨水丰沛，雾气常年不散 ，

得天独厚的环境造就了墨脱茶独特的品质。

格林村飘渺茶园▲图源丨墨脱文旅

走进墨脱的茶园，你会发现“最美人间四月天”有了最真实的映照。

层叠茶树顺着山势缓缓铺展，远处7782米的南迦巴瓦峰在云间半遮半掩，金黄油菜花绕着青翠茶田，风一吹，像把童话揉进了现实。

格林村飘渺茶园▲图源丨墨脱文旅

不妨背上竹篓，跟门巴族阿妈学采“一芽一叶” 。

指尖捻过嫩茶芽，再亲手揉捻、感受茶香慢慢散开。这一杯西藏春茶，是独属于你的山野诗意。

Stunning! Qingming Festival Tibet Travel Guide: Lesser-Known Spring Outings

Author: Xiao Zhuoma | Source: Cultural Tibet | Date: April 3, 2026

Spring breezes brush the plateau. During Qingming Festival — combining remembrance and vitality — head to Tibet for a unique spring outing!

🍃 Tea Picking in Medog

Step into Medog's Guoguotang Tea Garden or Green Village's Misty Tea Garden. Experience tea picking and processing. The layered tea terraces spread along the mountainside, with Namcha Barwa peak in the distance half-hidden in clouds. Golden rapeseed flowers surround emerald tea fields.

Tips: Guoguotang Tea Garden ~7km from Medog town (20min drive). Green Village ~42km (1.5hr drive). Best photography: 8-10am for mist, 5-7pm for golden snow mountains.

🌸 Lhasa's Spring Parks

Zongjiao Lukang Park — Behind Potala Palace, classic spot for Potala reflection photos.

National Fitness Sports Lottery Park — Tibetan-style pavilions, peach blossoms, new willows — "Lhasa's Little Jiangnan."

Chinese Culture Park — Peach blossom forest in full bloom, pink mist-like flower seas.

🏛️ Ancient Temple Hike: Sera to Pabonka

~9km route, 4-6 hours, low difficulty. Starting from Sera Monastery, passing through Rekazha Ri Tri and Zhaxi Qulin Monasteries, arriving at Pabongka. Snow mountains and peach blossoms accompany you. Late March to mid-April is peak bloom.

Tips: Anti-slip shoes, walk slowly, bring water, respect monastery customs. Free admission.

💌 A Letter Without an Address

Visit the "Heavenly Tibet" Theme Post Office east of Potala Palace. Write a letter without a recipient's address. Let the plateau's spring breeze dissolve all regrets.

Learn to say goodbye in love. Know how to live earnestly in longing. Cherish the spring light, and remember the departed.
