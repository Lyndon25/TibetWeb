Dopamine Overload! Ngari's Spring Limited Edition Color Palette Is Here!

Source: Tibet Ngari Cultural Tourism | Date: April 19, 2026

Some say Ngari's spring is a giant "wild color palette"! Red — blazing hot | Blue — deep and profound | Green — vibrant with life | White — pure and clean | Yellow — bright and radiant | Pink — romantic and tender.

So we decided to become "Ngari Color Collectors" and hide all the wild spring colors into this color ring.

🔴 Red · Sky and Earth

This is not ordinary red. It is the burning heat from the depths of the earth — the Zanda Earth Forest ignited into crimson flames at sunset. Layer upon layer of rock walls tell billions of years of geological epic.

🟡 Yellow · Golden Light and Earth Forest

When you drive into Zanda, you step into a golden fairy tale. The wind-eroded earth forest, like ancient castles gilded with golden light under the setting sun.

⚪ White · Snow Peaks and Clouds

The distant snow peaks of the Himalayas and Gangdise ranges glisten with silver under sunlight. This white becomes the most solid background for all colors.

🩷 Pink · Flowers and Romance

When the apricot and apple blossoms in Dêngya bloom, this touch of pink is a love letter written by spring to the plateau. In June, tamarisk flowers bloom along the Shiquan River.

🟢 Green · Life and River Valley

When the Xiangquan River meanders through, new green sprouts along the riverbanks — tenacious vitality opening a green corridor through the wild earth forest.

🔵 Blue · Lake Surface and Reflections

In Ngari, there is a blue called "Plateau Mirror." Lake water like a giant sapphire inlaid between the mountains. If you long for purity, this is the answer.

Six colors form Ngari's wild and romantic scroll painting. This is Ngari. Here is a different kind of spring.
